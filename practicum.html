Company Overview

OGIS Philippines, Inc. is an IT solutions provider located at The Valero Tower, 112 Valero St., Salcedo Village, Makati City. Formerly known as Fasttrack Solutions Inc., it was acquired and rebranded in 2024 under the Osaka Gas Group. The company specializes in ERP implementation, cloud services, and data analytics across various industries in Southeast Asia.

Nature of Work / Tasks Given

I was assigned to the Business Analytics team, where I worked on developing interactive dashboards and reports using Power BI. My tasks involved data cleaning, transformation, writing DAX measures, and creating visuals to support data monitoring. I also completed a case study that simulated real-world sales reporting, helping consolidate and analyze business data across multiple sources.

Total Hours Rendered

Over the course of the internship, I rendered a total of 350 hours from May 12, 2025 to June 10, 2025. The hours were distributed across three structured phases. Phase 1 focused on general IT and soft skills, accounting for 48 hours of foundational training. Phase 2 involved product-specific lessons in data science, SAP, and Power BI, with a total of 62 hours dedicated to technical development. The final and most extensive phase, Phase 3, consisted of 240 hours of hands-on work immersion, where I applied my training to real-world tasks, dashboard development, and project-based outputs.

Conclusion

The internship at OGIS Philippines, Inc. was a valuable experience that allowed me to apply both my technical knowledge and soft skills in a real-world setting. Through structured training and hands-on projects, I gained a deeper understanding of business analytics, data visualization, and workplace professionalism. This practicum has not only strengthened my capabilities in tools like Power BI but also prepared me to confidently take on future roles in the field of data and technology.
